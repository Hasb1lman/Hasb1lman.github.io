Step by step how to make a halal burrito

  • Cook the ground beef or boneless chicken until browned
  • Mix all the ingredients except the cheese and the tortillas
  • You can use ketchup or mustard, put the ketchup on the tortilla and then spread
  • Put the cheese on the tortillas
  • And then put all the ingredients on the tortilla
  • Then roll up the tortillas
  • And then toast the burrito on a pan or you can use oven
  • Your halal mexican burrito is ready to serve
