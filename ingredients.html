How to make Halal original mexican Burrito.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans
  • 500g ground beef or boneless chicken
  • A few chopped mild green chillies
  • 1 onion, chopped fine
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 8 bag shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 burrito-size halal flour tortillas
